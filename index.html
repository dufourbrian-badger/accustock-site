The Problem

Shopify doesn't link shared inventory

If you sell the same product as singles, 3-packs, and gift sets, Shopify tracks each variant separately. That means oversells, angry customers, and hours of manual spreadsheet work.

📈

Overselling

A customer orders a 3-pack but your singles already claimed that stock. Shopify doesn't know, and now you're short.

🕒

Manual reconciliation

You burn time every day updating spreadsheets just to figure out your real stock. Every. Single. Day.

😱

No audit trail

When the numbers don't match, there's no easy way to trace what changed, when, or why.

Features

Everything you need to trust your numbers

📦

Item-Based Tracking

Define your real physical items and link them to any number of Shopify variants. Set how many units each variant uses — like "3-pack uses 3" — and let AccuStock handle the math.

⚡

Automatic Adjustments

Orders, cancellations, and refunds are handled automatically via Shopify webhooks — and the corrected availability syncs back to your storefront, with no manual work.

📋

Complete Audit Trail

Every stock change is logged with timestamps and before/after values — including edits you make directly in Shopify. Trace any discrepancy back to its exact source: order, refund, or manual change.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

See all your item stock at a glance with low-stock alerts. Know exactly where you stand without opening a spreadsheet.

🌎

Multi-Location Support

Track inventory across multiple Shopify locations. AccuStock manages stock per-location so your fulfillment stays accurate everywhere.

🛡

Coverage View

See at a glance which variants aren't linked to an item yet, so you can connect them before they oversell. Nothing slips through unprotected.
