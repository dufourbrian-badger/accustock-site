1. Description of Service

AccuStock is an inventory management application for Shopify merchants. The App provides component-based inventory tracking, allowing merchants to define physical items ("components"), link them to Shopify product variants with consumption ratios, and automatically adjust stock levels when orders are placed, cancelled, or refunded. The App also provides an audit trail of all inventory changes, a stock dashboard, and variant coverage reporting.
