The Problem

Shopify doesn't link shared inventory

If you sell the same product as singles, 3-packs, and gift sets, Shopify tracks each variant separately. That means oversells, angry customers, and hours of manual spreadsheet work.

📈

Overselling

A customer orders a 3-pack but your singles already claimed that stock. Shopify doesn't know, and now you're short.

🕒

Manual reconciliation

Merchants spend 30-60 minutes daily updating spreadsheets to figure out real stock. Every. Single. Day.

😱

No audit trail

When numbers don't match, there's no way to trace what happened. Shopify's history caps at 250 entries.

Features

Everything you need to trust your numbers

📦

Component-Based Tracking

Define your real physical items and link them to any number of Shopify variants. Set consumption ratios like "3-pack uses 3 units" and let AccuStock handle the math.

⚡

Automatic Adjustments

Orders, cancellations, and refunds are processed instantly via Shopify webhooks. Stock levels update in seconds, not hours.

📋

Complete Audit Trail

Every single stock change is logged with timestamps, before/after values, and source info. Trace any discrepancy back to the exact order.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

See all your component stock at a glance with low-stock alerts. Know exactly where you stand without opening a spreadsheet.

🌎

Multi-Location Support

Track inventory across multiple Shopify locations. AccuStock manages stock per-location so your fulfillment stays accurate everywhere.

🔒

Shopify Storefront Sync

Corrected quantities are pushed back to Shopify automatically. Your customers always see accurate "Add to Cart" availability.
